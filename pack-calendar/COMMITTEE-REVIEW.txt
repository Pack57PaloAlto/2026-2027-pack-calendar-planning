PLANNING PRINCIPLES
-------------------
  - Pack meetings: 3rd Thursday of the month (adjusted when conflicts arise)
  - Pack committee meetings: 1st Thursday of the month (virtual)
    (Stanford District Roundtable is the same evening)
  - Avoids: PAUSD no-school days, major religious holidays across all faiths,
    private school breaks, long weekends when families travel
  - Aligned with Pacific Skyline Council / Stanford District events
  - Season: September 2026 through May 2027
